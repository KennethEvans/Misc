Call History

This application shows information about the call history on your device. The calls displayed can be filtered, and the history can be saved to disk. The data are in the form of a ListView. Clicking on an item in the ListView will give further information about that call.

Menu Items

Refresh

Refreshes the information.

Save To SD Card

Saves the information to the SD card. The file is a tab-delimited CSV file named CallHistory.yyyy-MM-dd-HHmmss.csv and appears in the Misc directory of the SD card. (yyyy is the year; MM is the month; dd is the day; HH is the hour; mm is the minute; and ss is the second, so the files should sort ordered by the time they were created.)

Filter

Allows you to set filters on what information is displayed. The filters are built-in and cannot currently be customized .

Help

Displays this page.
